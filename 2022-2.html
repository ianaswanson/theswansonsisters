Healthy Habit Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup butter
  • 3/4 Cup sugar
  • 1 Cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 T water
  • 2 T flaxseed meal
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 t baking soda
  • 1 t
  • 3 Cups oats
  • 1 bag chocolate chips
  • 1 t cinnamon


Directions:

    1.    Preheat oven to 350.

    2.    Mix the flaxseed meal and water and let set for 3-5 min.

    3.    Beat butter, sugar, and brown sugar well.

    4.    Add eggs and mix well.

    5.    Add flaxseed mix and vanilla, and beat well.

    6.    Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt.

    7.    Add dry ingredients to butter mix.

    8.    Stir in oats and chips. 

    9.    Scoop onto a greased baking sheet.

    10.    Bake for 12 minutes. 

    11.    Let set for a couple of minutes then remove from tray.

Healthy Habit Cookies

Delicious oatmeal chocolate chip cookies with a healthy twist

Cookie Details

Year 2022
Type Oatmeal

Eliza's Review

"Can I eat them for breakfast?"

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup butter
  • 3/4 Cup sugar
  • 1 Cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 T water
  • 2 T flaxseed meal
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 t baking soda
  • 1 t salt
  • 3 Cups oats
  • 1 bag chocolate chips
  • 1 t cinnamon

Instructions

1

Preheat oven

Preheat oven to 350.

2

Prepare flaxseed

Mix the flaxseed meal and water and let set for 3-5 min.

3

Cream butter and sugars

Beat butter, sugar, and brown sugar well.

4

Add eggs

Add eggs and mix well.

5

Add flaxseed and vanilla

Add flaxseed mix and vanilla, and beat well.

6

Sift dry ingredients

Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt.

7

Combine

Add dry ingredients to butter mix.

8

Add oats and chips

Stir in oats and chips.

9

Scoop onto baking sheet

Scoop onto a greased baking sheet.

10

Bake

Bake for 12 minutes.

11

Cool

Let set for a couple of minutes then remove from tray.
